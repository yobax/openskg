# Download Notes

The repository structure has been generated with official source links.

In this ChatGPT runtime, direct PDF downloads from external websites failed due DNS/network restrictions. Therefore, PDF files are not bundled in this zip.

To download official public PDFs on your Mac:

```bash
cd Security-Frameworks-v2
bash 00_Index/Sources/download-official-documents.sh
```

Some documents may require registration, login, purchase, or license acceptance. Do not redistribute paid or restricted standards.

## Documents expected to download by script

- IPA SME Security Guideline v4.0
- FSA Financial Sector Cybersecurity Guideline
- METI Cybersecurity Management Guideline v3.0
- IPA Practice Collection for METI Guideline
- PrivacyMark PMS Construction / Operation Guideline
- ISMAP public sample documents
- National Cybersecurity Strategy 2025
- NIST CSF 2.0
- GDPR official EU regulation PDF

## Documents intentionally not bundled

- ISO/IEC standards: generally paid/licensed
- CIS Controls: official download may require form submission
- SOC 2 detailed criteria: use AICPA official materials and licensed reports
- CSA CAIQ / CCM: official download terms may apply
- PCI DSS: use PCI SSC official site and comply with terms
